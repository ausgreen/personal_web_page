Calculus Single Variable Part 3 – Coursera – University of Pennsylvania

I already took the calculus series through differential equations, but after four years of only applying concepts of calculus I decided it was time for some remediation. UPenn has put together this very rigorous and fast paced course which takes a unique approach to differentiation and integration by first understanding Series. I would really recommend this MOOC to anyone who needs a calculus refresher
